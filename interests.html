Introduction to Information Technology

Interests in I.T.

What is your interest in IT? When did your interest in IT start? Was there a particular event or person that sparked your interest? Outline your IT experience (if any).

My interest in IT started off when I was young from watching my Uncle play video games, discovering how easy it was to communicate to your friends from the internet using communication programs such as MSN Messenger and playing my first MMORPG playing with my friends and sharing great memories. My experience in IT is minimal apart from enjoying it but have tried to create websites which made me learn basics of HTML and CSS which then developed slightly when MySpace came to become popular.

Why did you choose to come to RMIT?

I chose RMIT as it was through Open Universities Australia and 100% online study. As I am a working Dad working in retail, it is hard to find the time to attend a university whilst still trying to provide for my family. Thankfully RMIT is one of the few universities that offer the I.T. course. The other courses that were also listed on the Open Universities Australia in the I.T section were not as direct in the one field and offered the great courses RMIT offer.

What do you expect to learn during your studies?

In my studies I expect to learn how to design web pages, learn how to code in a professional manner, how to act in a professional I.T environment and to learn as much as I can about the subject that I am passionate about. I wish to have all these skills not just to find suitable employment, but also to start a hobby of programming and possible freelance work.
